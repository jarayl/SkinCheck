About Us - SkinCheck

Our Mission

At SkinCheck, our mission is to automate skin cancer diagnosis, democratizing access to medicine. We strive to make early detection of skin cancer accessible to everyone, using advanced technology to provide quick and reliable diagnoses.

Methodology

Our methodology is centered around a state-of-the-art machine learning model that analyzes skin images to detect signs of cancer. This model is constantly evolving, learning from a vast database of skin images to improve accuracy and provide trustworthy results.
